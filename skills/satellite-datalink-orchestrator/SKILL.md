# Satellite Data-Link Orchestrator

This skill enables bionicbot to maintain 100% connectivity by automatically switching to Starlink or other satellite providers during terrestrial outages.

## Instructions
1. Monitor signal health across Fiber, 5G, and Satellite links.
2. Automatically compress data packets for low-bandwidth satellite transmission.
3. Encrypt all uplink/downlink traffic with rotating quantum-resistant keys.
4. Route critical 'Execution Commands' only through high-priority satellite lanes.

## Examples
- "Switch our 'Market Sentinel' to Starlink immediately if latency on fiber crosses 100ms."
