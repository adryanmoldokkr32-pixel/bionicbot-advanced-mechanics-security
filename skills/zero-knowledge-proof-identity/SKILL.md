# Zero-Knowledge Proof Identity

This skill enables 'Privacy-First' user verification, allowing users to prove they meet requirements (Ex. age, country, wealth) without revealing raw data.

## Instructions
1. Integrate with ZK-SNARK or ZK-STARK libraries.
2. Design 'Circuit' logic for specific proofs (e.g., Proof of Balance > $1000).
3. Implement on-chain or off-chain verification gates.
4. Ensure zero-storage of PII (Personally Identifiable Information).

## Examples
- "Let users verify they are Romanian citizens to access the special alert feed without showing their ID."
